Format v1

Volt PCB JSON is canonical board state plus a disposable viewer cache.

Format: volt.pcb

Version: 1

Scope: placement-ready PCB projection over one logical circuit

Ownership

The board projects existing logical connectivity.

PCB JSON stores board geometry, layer metadata, physical features, cached footprint geometry, and component placements. It references components, pins, and nets from the bound logical circuit; it never creates or mutates logical connectivity.

Canonical

Loaded into the kernel

Board name, units, layers, layer stack, outline, features, footprint definitions, and placements are rebuilt as volt::Board state.

Derived

Checked, then recomputed

Viewer pad resolutions and diagnostics are validated on read, but are not trusted as source data. The returned board recomputes them from selected parts and cached footprints.

Out of scope

No manufacturing truth

Gerbers, autorouting, KiCad PCB import/export, simulation, and full 3D editing are later adapters or layers.

Field order is deterministic. Entity arrays use insertion order, and local IDs use stable prefixes such as board_layer:, board_feature:, footprint_def:, footprint_pad:, and component_placement:.

Reader checks

Malformed structure fails at the load boundary.

The reader rejects duplicate or non-sequential local IDs, dangling layer-stack entries, placements that reference missing components, footprint references that do not match selected physical parts, and viewer pad resolutions with dangling placement, pad, pin, or net references.

  • Component references are checked against the bound Circuit.
  • Footprint references are checked against cached footprint definitions and selected-part footprint refs.
  • Pad projection refs are checked against footprint pads and recomputed selected-part pin-pad mappings.
  • Net refs are checked against the logical circuit and must match the resolved pin's current net.
  • Diagnostics serialize reporting refs for highlighting, including PCB placement refs, without making EntityRef a traversal handle.
