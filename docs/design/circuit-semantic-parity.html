Compatibility and architecture proof

Compatibility means supported authoring and downstream behavior remain stable—not that transitional storage-shaped APIs remain public.

  • Public Circuit is locked to sixteen declarations: fifteen domain operations plus the deleted temporary-range overload.
  • No public subsystem container shell, shared subsystem storage adapter, or replacement facade family remains.
  • No public passkey, raw restore, per-table stable-ID getter/count, or ordinary compatibility lock remains.
  • The sole logical restore authority is a narrow private friend accepting a typed restoration plan.
  • The public snapshot and independent admission policy detect method/order/include drift, retired overloads, specialized root reads, and removed shell symbols.
